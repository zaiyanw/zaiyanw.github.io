Zaiyan Wei

Assistant Professor of Management

Biography

Zaiyan Wei (CV in PDF) is an assistant professor in the Krannert School of Management, Purdue University. He is also a faculty affiliate with the Krenicki Center and Purdue FinTech Center. Before joining Purdue, Zaiyan was a research associate at the University of Arizona.
