Zaiyan Wei

Assistant Professor of Management

Biography

Zaiyan Wei (CV on the Purdue page) is a tenure-track faculty member in the MIS Area of the Krannert School of Management, Purdue University. He is also a faculty affiliate with the Krenicki Center for Business Analytics & Machine Learning.

Interests

  • Economics of IS
  • FinTech
  • P2P/marketplace lending
  • Platform economics
  • Social media and networks
